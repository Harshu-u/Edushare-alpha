Why EduShare?

A Smarter Way to Study.

Find Gold, Not Garbage

Tired of scrolling through junk? Our community rates every note. Sort by top-rated to find the best, most-trusted materials instantly.

Search Smarter, Not Harder

Our blazing-fast search scans titles, descriptions, tags, and even uploader names. Find exactly what you need for 'DSA' or 'sem4' in seconds.

For Students, By Students

This isn't a corporate product. It's an open-source tool built to actually help you and your classmates succeed. No ads, no paywalls. Ever.

Earn Your Rank

Gain reputation points for every good note you upload and every helpful rating you give. Climb the leaderboard and become a top contributor.

Teacher & Admin Approved

This platform isn't the wild west. Teachers and staff can manage categories, verify notes, and remove low-quality content, ensuring the library stays clean and trustworthy.
